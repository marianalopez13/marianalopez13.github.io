About Me

As a reverent, respectful, and responsible sophomore at Excelsior Academy, I am working towards an Associates Degree in Applied Science, alongside my high school diploma. Excelsior Academy is a New York State P-TECH school, which is a four to six year program with an IT Program. With the help of my teachers, I went to Sacred Heart School with earned scholarships for the last couple of years before graduating from middle school. At my graduation from SHS, I earned scholarships from the American Girls Association and the Sight and Sound Club. I also earned the following awards: the President’s Award for Educational Excellence, the Honor Roll Award, the Citizenship Award, a Certificate of Appreciation for Achievement in Spanish, and a Certificate of Membership in the National Junior Honor Society. Apart from my academics in middle school, I participated in sports like basketball and cheerleading. I also participated in community service, which included making U.S. Navy Christmas bags, braiding church palms, Spanish tutoring, etc.. When in search for a high school, I was given the opportunity to join Excelsior Academy. Apart from school, I work currently at Talavera Restaurant helping my dad pursue his dream as a chef owner. At the age of 10, to show the superior care I have for the meaning of family, I volunteered summer vacation to go work with my mom as a caterer at Anthony’s Pier 9. The preceding school year, 2016-2017, I did Geometry tutoring and basketball. This year, I plan to keep excelling in my studies, for instance, AP Physics, College Success and Career Plan, and College Algebra/College Trigonometry. I also look forward to dedicating more time towards community service in the year 2017-2018.
