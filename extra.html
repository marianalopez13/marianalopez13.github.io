Extra-Curricular Activities

My name is Mariana Lopez, and I have lived in New York my entire life. I have experience working as a caterer at Anthony's Pier 9. Recently, I am now working as a waitress and cashier at my father's new restaurant. Some of the other work I have done includes community service. Out of all the community service I have done, I've best enjoyed making Christmas bags for the U.S. Navy. In eighth grade, I took my Algebra and Biology Regents. I am studying hard this year to ace my Geometry Regents, U.S. History Regents, and math accuplacer. I am also giving my best in all of my classes, because I want to do my best in the Excelsior P-Tech Program. I want to get the best possible education I can possibly get even if that means more challenges and hard work.
